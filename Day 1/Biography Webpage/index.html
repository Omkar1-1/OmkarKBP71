Albert Einstein

Early Life

Albert Einstein was born on March 14, 1879, in Ulm, Germany. He was a theoretical physicist who developed the theory of relativity.

Major Contribution

  • Theory of Relativity (E = mc2)
  • Quantum Mechanics
  • Nobel Prize in Physics (1921)

Personal Life

Einstein married Mileva Marić in 1903. They had two sons. He later married his cousin, Elsa Löwenthal.
